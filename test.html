About Me

I'm a results-driven backend software developer with a passion for solving complex technical challenges and creating impactful software solutions. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how things work, which led me to pursue a Master's in Computer Information Systems at Colorado State University. Since then, I've specialized in building scalable backend systems, RESTful APIs, and microservices architecture using Java and frameworks like Spring Boot.

With over 5 years of industry experience, I've worked on diverse projects across e-commerce, finance, and cloud-based platforms, helping companies design robust systems that are efficient, reliable, and user-friendly. My experience extends to AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes, empowering teams to deploy, scale, and manage applications with confidence.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new tech trends, mentoring aspiring developers, or indulging in my love for coffee and watching anime. I'm always excited to collaborate on meaningful projects that have the potential to make a real difference in the world.
